Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Service Commitment: Customer Mind IQ is committed to providing reliable, high-performance customer intelligence services with measurable uptime and response guarantees.

1. Service Availability Commitments

1.1 Uptime Guarantees

Service Tier Monthly Uptime Commitment Maximum Downtime per Month
Free Tier 99.0% 7 hours 18 minutes
Professional Tier 99.5% 3 hours 39 minutes
Enterprise Tier 99.9% 43 minutes

1.2 Service Availability Definition

Service availability is calculated as the percentage of time the Customer Mind IQ platform is operational and accessible to users during a calendar month, excluding:

  • Planned maintenance (with 48-hour advance notice)
  • Emergency maintenance (security patches, critical fixes)
  • Third-party service outages beyond our control
  • Issues caused by customer actions or configurations

2. Performance Commitments

2.1 Response Time Guarantees

Service Component Target Response Time Measurement Method
Dashboard Loading < 3 seconds 95th percentile
AI Analysis Processing < 30 seconds 95th percentile
Report Generation < 60 seconds 95th percentile
Data Export < 120 seconds 95th percentile

2.2 Data Processing Commitments

  • Real-time Analytics: Customer health scores updated every 5 minutes
  • Batch Processing: Daily analytical reports completed within 4 hours of scheduled time
  • Data Synchronization: Connected platform data synchronized within 15 minutes
  • Backup Frequency: Automated backups every 6 hours with 30-day retention

3. Support Response Commitments

3.1 Support Channels and Response Times

Tier Support Channels Critical Issues High Priority Normal Priority
Free Community Forum, Email N/A N/A 72 hours
Professional Email, In-app Chat 4 hours 24 hours 48 hours
Enterprise Phone, Email, Priority Chat 1 hour 8 hours 24 hours

3.2 Issue Classification

  • Critical: Service completely unavailable or major security issue
  • High Priority: Major feature not working, significant performance degradation
  • Normal Priority: Minor issues, feature requests, general questions

4. Security and Compliance Commitments

4.1 Security Standards

  • SOC 2 Type II compliance maintained and audited annually
  • ISO 27001 certification (planned for Q2 2025)
  • GDPR and CCPA compliance for all data processing
  • Regular third-party security assessments
  • Vulnerability scanning and penetration testing quarterly

4.2 Data Protection Commitments

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication for all admin accounts
  • Role-based access controls and audit logging
  • Data breach notification within 72 hours

5. Service Credits and Remedies

5.1 Service Credit Calculation

If we fail to meet our uptime commitments, you may be eligible for service credits:

Monthly Uptime Percentage Service Credit
99.0% - 99.5% 10% of monthly fees
95.0% - 99.0% 25% of monthly fees
< 95.0% 50% of monthly fees

5.2 Service Credit Process

  • Credits must be requested within 30 days of the affected period
  • Credits are applied to future invoices, not cash refunds
  • Credits are the sole remedy for SLA violations
  • Maximum credits per month cannot exceed 50% of monthly fees

6. Maintenance and Updates

6.1 Planned Maintenance

  • Notification: 48 hours advance notice via email and in-app notifications
  • Timing: Scheduled during low-usage periods (typically Sunday 2-6 AM UTC)
  • Duration: Planned maintenance windows limited to 4 hours maximum
  • Frequency: Regular maintenance scheduled monthly, major updates quarterly

6.2 Emergency Maintenance

  • Critical security patches may be applied with minimal notice
  • Emergency fixes for service-impacting issues
  • Notification provided as soon as operationally possible
  • Post-incident reports provided within 48 hours

7. Monitoring and Reporting

7.1 Service Monitoring

  • 24/7 automated monitoring of all service components
  • Real-time alerting for service degradations
  • Performance metrics tracked and logged
  • Public status page available at status.customermindiq.com

7.2 Monthly Service Reports

Professional and Enterprise customers receive monthly reports including:

  • Service availability statistics
  • Performance metrics and trends
  • Incident summaries and resolutions
  • Upcoming maintenance schedules

8. Limitations and Exclusions

8.1 SLA Exclusions

This SLA does not apply to service unavailability caused by:

  • Customer actions, configurations, or misuse of the service
  • Third-party services or integrations beyond our control
  • Internet connectivity issues not related to our infrastructure
  • Scheduled maintenance with proper advance notice
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, war, terrorism, etc.)
  • Denial of service attacks or other malicious activities

8.2 Service Level Measurements

Service levels are measured from our monitoring systems and infrastructure. Customer-specific network or device issues are not included in availability calculations.

9. Escalation Procedures

9.1 Support Escalation

  1. Level 1: Initial support team response
  2. Level 2: Senior technical specialist (if not resolved within SLA timeframe)
  3. Level 3: Engineering team and management (critical issues)
  4. Executive Escalation: Available for Enterprise customers

9.2 SLA Dispute Resolution

SLA disputes will be handled through our standard support channels. If not resolved within 30 days, disputes may be escalated to executive management.

10. Service Improvements

10.1 Continuous Improvement

  • Regular review of SLA metrics and customer feedback
  • Investment in infrastructure and technology improvements
  • Proactive capacity planning and scaling
  • Annual SLA review and updates based on service evolution

11. Changes to This SLA

We may update this SLA to reflect service improvements or changes. Material changes will be communicated 60 days in advance via email and platform notifications.
