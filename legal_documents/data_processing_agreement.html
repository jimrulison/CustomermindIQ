Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Important: This Data Processing Agreement governs the processing of personal data by Customer Mind IQ on behalf of its customers in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Definitions

  • "Controller": The customer who determines the purposes and means of processing personal data
  • "Processor": Customer Mind IQ, which processes personal data on behalf of the Controller
  • "Personal Data": Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
  • "Processing": Any operation performed on personal data
  • "Data Subject": The individual whose personal data is being processed
  • "Sub-processor": Third-party service providers engaged by Customer Mind IQ

2. Scope and Applicability

2.1. This DPA applies to all personal data processed by Customer Mind IQ as part of providing the Service.

2.2. This DPA supplements our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

2.3. In case of conflict, this DPA takes precedence over other agreements regarding data processing.

3. Roles and Responsibilities

3.1 Controller Responsibilities (You)

  • Determine the purposes and means of processing personal data
  • Ensure lawful basis for processing exists
  • Obtain necessary consents from data subjects
  • Provide data subject notices and privacy information
  • Respond to data subject requests
  • Ensure data accuracy and completeness

3.2 Processor Responsibilities (Customer Mind IQ)

  • Process personal data only as instructed by the Controller
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures
  • Assist with data subject requests
  • Notify of data breaches within 72 hours
  • Maintain records of processing activities
  • Delete or return data upon termination

4. Processing Details

Aspect Details
Purpose Customer intelligence analysis, behavioral insights, marketing automation, revenue analytics
Categories of Data Customer identifiers, contact information, transaction data, behavioral data, engagement metrics
Data Subjects End customers of the Controller, website visitors, marketing prospects
Duration Duration of the service agreement plus 30 days for data return/deletion

5. Security Measures

5.1 Technical Measures

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Automated backup and disaster recovery

5.2 Organizational Measures

  • Staff training on data protection principles
  • Confidentiality agreements for all employees
  • Incident response procedures
  • Regular security awareness training
  • Vendor management and due diligence

6.2 Sub-processor Changes

We will notify you 30 days before adding new sub-processors. You may object within 30 days if the change creates unacceptable risk.

7. Data Subject Rights

7.1 Assistance with Data Subject Requests

We will assist you in responding to data subject requests including:

  • Access requests (provide data export functionality)
  • Rectification requests (provide data editing capabilities)
  • Erasure requests (provide data deletion functionality)
  • Portability requests (provide data export in machine-readable format)
  • Restriction requests (provide processing limitation controls)

7.2 Response Timeline

We will assist with data subject requests within 10 business days of receiving your request.

8. Data Breach Notification

8.1 Notification Requirements

  • We will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of a data breach
  • Notification will include available details about the breach
  • We will provide ongoing updates as more information becomes available
  • We will assist with breach notifications to data subjects if required

8.2 Breach Response

  • Immediate containment and investigation
  • Assessment of impact and risk to data subjects
  • Implementation of remedial measures
  • Documentation of breach response activities

9. Data Transfers

9.1 International Transfers

Personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EEA. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Binding Corporate Rules where applicable
  • Additional safeguards as required by law

10. Audits and Compliance

10.1 Audit Rights

You have the right to conduct audits of our data processing activities, subject to:

  • Reasonable advance notice (30 days minimum)
  • Execution of confidentiality agreements
  • Coordination with our security and compliance teams
  • Coverage of reasonable audit costs

10.2 Compliance Certifications

We maintain relevant compliance certifications and will provide copies upon request:

  • SOC 2 Type II
  • ISO 27001 (planned)
  • GDPR compliance attestations

11. Data Return and Deletion

11.1 End of Processing

Upon termination of the service agreement or upon your request:

  • We will return all personal data in a commonly used format
  • We will securely delete all personal data from our systems
  • You have 30 days from termination to request data return
  • After 30 days, data will be automatically deleted

11.2 Exceptions

Data may be retained longer if required by applicable law or legitimate business interests (such as financial record keeping requirements).

12. Liability and Indemnification

12.1 Mutual Cooperation

Both parties agree to cooperate in good faith to address any data protection issues that may arise.

12.2 Limitation of Liability

Our liability for data processing is limited as set forth in the main Terms of Service, except where prohibited by applicable law.

13. Term and Termination

13.1. This DPA remains in effect for the duration of our service agreement.

13.2. Either party may terminate this DPA with 30 days' written notice.

13.3. Provisions related to data return, deletion, and confidentiality survive termination.

14. Amendments

This DPA may only be amended by written agreement of both parties, except for updates required to maintain compliance with applicable laws.
