Acceptable Use Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Important: This Acceptable Use Policy defines the permitted uses of Customer Mind IQ and the behaviors that are prohibited. Violation of this policy may result in account suspension or termination.

1. Purpose and Scope

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of Customer Mind IQ's platform and services. This policy applies to all users, including free, professional, and enterprise customers. By using our services, you agree to comply with this AUP.

2. Permitted Uses

Customer Mind IQ is designed for legitimate business intelligence and customer analytics purposes. Permitted uses include:

  • Analyzing your own customer data for business insights
  • Creating marketing campaigns based on customer intelligence
  • Monitoring customer health and engagement metrics
  • Generating reports and analytics for business decision-making
  • Integrating with your existing business platforms and tools
  • Sharing insights within your organization or with authorized partners
  • Using AI-powered features for customer behavior analysis
  • Automating customer engagement and retention workflows
  • Conducting A/B tests and optimization experiments
  • Accessing our APIs for legitimate business purposes

3. Prohibited Activities

3.1 Illegal and Unlawful Activities

  • Using the service for any illegal, fraudulent, or unauthorized purposes
  • Violating any applicable laws, regulations, or third-party rights
  • Engaging in money laundering, terrorist financing, or other criminal activities
  • Using the service to facilitate illegal gambling or unlicensed financial services
  • Impersonating any person or entity, or falsely representing your affiliation

3.2 Data and Privacy Violations

  • Processing personal data without proper legal basis or consent
  • Violating GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable data protection laws
  • Collecting, storing, or processing sensitive personal data inappropriately
  • Sharing customer data with unauthorized third parties
  • Using data obtained through our service for identity theft or fraud
  • Circumventing data protection measures or access controls

3.3 Spam and Unsolicited Communications

  • Sending spam, bulk emails, or unsolicited commercial communications
  • Using the platform to conduct email marketing without proper opt-in consent
  • Bypassing unsubscribe mechanisms or continuing to contact opted-out recipients
  • Creating or distributing chain letters, pyramid schemes, or similar content
  • Using purchased, scraped, or third-party email lists without proper authorization

3.4 Technical Abuse and Security

  • Attempting to gain unauthorized access to our systems or other users' accounts
  • Reverse engineering, decompiling, or disassembling our software
  • Introducing malware, viruses, or other harmful code
  • Conducting distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks
  • Attempting to bypass security measures or authentication systems
  • Using automated tools to scrape or harvest data from our platform
  • Overloading our servers or networks with excessive requests

3.5 Intellectual Property Violations

  • Infringing on copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights
  • Uploading or sharing copyrighted content without proper authorization
  • Using our platform to distribute pirated software or media
  • Claiming ownership of our intellectual property or branding
  • Creating derivative works based on our platform without permission

3.6 Competitive and Business Interference

  • Using our service to develop competing products or services
  • Reselling or redistributing our services without proper authorization
  • Benchmarking our performance for competitive intelligence
  • Attempting to recruit our employees or contractors
  • Interfering with other users' use of the platform

3.7 Content and Communication Standards

  • Posting or transmitting offensive, obscene, or inappropriate content
  • Engaging in harassment, bullying, or threatening behavior
  • Discriminating against individuals based on protected characteristics
  • Promoting violence, hatred, or harmful activities
  • Sharing false, misleading, or defamatory information

4. Account Sharing and Security

4.1 Account Responsibility

  • You are responsible for all activities that occur under your account
  • You must maintain the security and confidentiality of your login credentials
  • You must notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account
  • You may not share your account credentials with others

4.2 Multiple Accounts

  • Free tier users may not maintain multiple accounts
  • Paid customers may have multiple accounts with proper business justification
  • Using multiple accounts to circumvent usage limits is prohibited

5. Resource Usage and Limits

5.1 Fair Use

You must use our resources fairly and reasonably:

  • Respect API rate limits and usage quotas
  • Avoid excessive server resource consumption
  • Use caching and optimization best practices
  • Report any suspected abuse or anomalous usage

6. Data Processing and Compliance

6.1 Your Responsibilities

  • Ensure you have proper legal basis for processing personal data
  • Obtain necessary consents and provide appropriate privacy notices
  • Comply with all applicable data protection laws and regulations
  • Implement appropriate security measures for sensitive data
  • Honor data subject rights and deletion requests

6.2 Prohibited Data Types

You may not process the following types of data through our platform:

  • Payment card information (PCI data) without proper compliance
  • Protected health information (PHI) without BAA agreement
  • Social security numbers or government identification numbers
  • Biometric data or genetic information
  • Data of children under 13 without parental consent
  • Any data obtained through illegal or unauthorized means

7. Third-Party Integrations

7.1 Integration Compliance

  • You must comply with third-party platform terms of service
  • Obtain proper authorization before connecting external accounts
  • Respect API rate limits and usage restrictions of connected platforms
  • Do not use integrations to violate third-party policies

7.2 Data Synchronization

  • Ensure you have rights to sync and process connected platform data
  • Monitor sync processes to prevent unauthorized data access
  • Disconnect integrations when no longer needed or authorized

8. Enforcement and Violations

8.1 Monitoring and Detection

We may monitor usage patterns and investigate potential policy violations through:

  • Automated systems and alerts
  • User reports and complaints
  • Manual reviews and audits
  • Third-party reports and law enforcement requests

8.2 Violation Response Process

Warning: Policy violations may result in immediate action without prior notice, depending on severity.

Level 1: Minor Violations

  • Written warning and guidance
  • Required policy acknowledgment
  • Mandatory compliance training
  • Temporary feature restrictions

Level 2: Moderate Violations

  • Temporary account suspension (7-30 days)
  • Service restrictions or limitations
  • Required remediation actions
  • Compliance monitoring period

Level 3: Severe Violations

  • Immediate account suspension or termination
  • Data export window before deletion
  • Legal action if applicable
  • Reporting to relevant authorities

8.3 Appeals Process

If you believe enforcement action was taken in error:

  • Submit an appeal within 30 days of the action
  • Provide detailed explanation and supporting evidence
  • We will review and respond within 15 business days
  • Decisions may be escalated to management if necessary

9. Reporting Violations

9.1 How to Report

If you become aware of policy violations:

  • Email: abuse@customermindiq.com
  • Support Portal: Submit a "Policy Violation" ticket
  • Emergency Security Issues: security@customermindiq.com

9.2 Report Information

When reporting violations, please provide:

  • Detailed description of the violation
  • Evidence or supporting documentation
  • User account or organization involved
  • Date and time of the incident
  • Any impact or harm caused

10. Legal Compliance

10.1 Cooperation with Authorities

  • We cooperate with law enforcement and regulatory authorities
  • We may disclose information as required by valid legal process
  • We report suspected criminal activity to appropriate authorities
  • We maintain records as required by applicable laws

10.2 International Compliance

  • You must comply with laws in your jurisdiction
  • Export control and sanctions laws apply to platform usage
  • Data residency requirements must be respected
  • Cross-border data transfers must comply with applicable laws

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in laws or regulations
  • New platform features or capabilities
  • Emerging security threats or abuse patterns
  • User feedback and community input

Material changes will be communicated 30 days in advance via email and platform notifications.
